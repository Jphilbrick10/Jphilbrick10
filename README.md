## Coherence Energy Labs

Independent research laboratory founded in 2024 by **Josh Philbrick**, developing a unified field theory (coherence field theory) and the **Coherence Lang** programming language — with an emphasis on results that are reproducible and verifiable: bit-for-bit reproducible across hardware, cryptographically signed, and independently re-runnable.

- **[Ariadne](https://github.com/Jphilbrick10/Ariadne)** — CR3BP / cislunar mission design & trans-Neptunian-object discovery, cross-validated against NASA GMAT
